AcadX Trust is a non profit charitable organisation started in the year 2017, with a noble intention of enhancing academic outcomes through the state of the art academic excellence automation software. The software platform allows collaborative environment for continuous improvement in performance and skills of students and teacher / faculty in accordance with the accreditation standard.

The idea of academic excellence automation was born when some experienced professors and industry experts felt the deep rooted gap in the education system of the country and must be addressed systematically. According to a recent report by a reputed magazine, there are about 33,000 colleges in India granting degrees. Millions of graduates are released in to the job market every year.
However only about 10% of them have strong understanding of basic concepts and suitable technical and soft skills to be employed in core companies and organizations and are able to contribute to progress of the nation. What can happens to the large proportion of remaining graduates. The dismal state must be addressed systematically, if not it may cause serious instability in economic and social conditions of the country.

AcadX team has a right blend of experienced academicians, industry experts and volunteers, their strong domain expertise and understanding led to the concept of academic excellence which aims to improve performance and skills of millions of students and academicians of the country.
