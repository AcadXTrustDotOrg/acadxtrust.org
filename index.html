UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION

AcadX Trust, established in 2017, is a non profit charitable organization dedicated to academic excellence by developing an advanced collaborative software platform for syllabus based educational contents, higher order evaluations, industry relevant skill trainings and for the management of projects, research and consultancy activities in accordance with the guidelines of accreditation boards. The platform also covers extracurricular activities of students inorder to ensure holistic development.

The software platform and contents contributed by volunteers are provided to students and teachers free of charge. Our programmes enable maximum development of intellectual capacities and skills of students and teachers in service to humanity and nation.

NATION BUILDING PROGRAMMES

ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE PROGRAMME

Freely available academic excellence software platform for college/university students and teachers to help improving their academic performance and skills for the prosperity of nation. Certain software modules support government and state board school students.

FOUNDATION PROGRAMME

Freely available analytics for government school students of class 8 to 10 to help improving their performance and skills at foundation level.
